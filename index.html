Rexin Material

Rexin is a synthetic leather-like material used for car seats due to its durability, water resistance, and affordable cost. It's ideal for everyday vehicles and provides a smooth, stylish finish.

Softlawn / Velvet

Softlawn or velvet material offers a luxurious feel and soft texture. Best suited for premium interiors, it gives a plush seating experience and adds elegance to any car.

Art Leather

Art leather mimics the look and feel of genuine leather but is more affordable and low maintenance. It’s perfect for customers seeking a balance of luxury and value.
